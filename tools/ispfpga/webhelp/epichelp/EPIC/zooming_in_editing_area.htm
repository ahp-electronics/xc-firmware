Zooming in the Main View

There are several tools you can use to zoom in and out of the EPIC layout, including the zoom toolbar, the mouse and mouse wheel, and keyboard combinations.

Zoom Toolbar

The following zoom commands are available on the EPIC toolbar and in the View menu.

Zoom In – enlarges the view of the entire layout.

Zoom Out – reduces the view of the entire layout.

Zoom Fit – reduces or enlarges the entire layout so that it fits inside the window.

Zoom To – enlarges the size of one or more selected sites, components, or connections on the layout and fills the window with the selection.

Zooming In and Out of an Area

You can enlarge an area of the layout by holding the left mouse button and dragging to the right and downward. Afterwards, you can zoom out of the area by dragging upward and to the left.

Zooming with the Mouse Wheel

The mouse wheel gives you finer zoom control, enabling you to zoom in or out in small increments. While pressing the Ctrl key, move the mouse wheel forward to zoom in and backward to zoom out.

Zooming with Keyboard Shortcuts

The following keyboard shortcuts enable you to instantly zoom in or out from your keyboard:

Zoom In – press Ctrl++ or press “i”
Zoom Out – press Ctrl+- or press “o”
