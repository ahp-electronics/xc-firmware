Swapping Components

The components to be swapped must be placed components of the same type.

To swap the locations of two components:
1. Select the components that you want to swap.
2. Choose Edit > Swap or click the swap button on the Actions panel.
3. Click OK.
The two components are unrouted, and then they swap locations and are rerouted.
