syn_hier attribute

syn_hier attribute controls the amount of hierarchical transformation across boundaries on module or component instances during optimization. You can apply syn_hier globally, or to a module.

Values:

soft: Tool determines the best optimization across hierarchical boundaries.

firm: Preserves the interface of the design unit, when there is cellpacking across the boundary, it changes the interface.

hard: Preserves the interface of the design unit and prevents most optimizations across the hierarchy.

fixed: Preserves the interface of the design unit with no exceptions and prevents all optimizations performed across hierarchical boundaries.

remove: Removes the level of hierarchy for the design unit. This only affects synthesis optimization. The hierarchy is reconstructed in the netlist and Technology view schematics.

macro: Preserves the interface and contents of the design with no exceptions.

flatten: Flattens the hierarchy of all levels in the design. This only affects synthesis optimization. The hierarchy is reconstructed in the netlist and Technology view schematics.
